Word from our founder

I believe in education as weapon for vulnerable youth to overcome the cycle of poverty and become leaders of positive change.

Hocet Tanzania is running its own boarding secondary school in Mkuranga Coast Region of Tanzania. The shool gives access to vulnerable youth to be educated and learn important skills free of charge.

To differentiate itself from many school and ensure employment to its graduates. Hocet run a mandatory extra curricula activities in entrepreneurship and employability its student beneficiaries.

Regardless the impacts Hocet Tanzania have had to the lives of many youth and children of Tanzania. Our founder father's goal is to build self sustainable school like the one in Mkuranga Tanzania the country.

Founder & Managing Director, Mr Hezekia Mwalugaja
