ABOUT HANANASIF ORPHANAGE CENTER - HOCET

Hananasif Orpahanage Center in Tanzania (Hocet) is a non profit organization on the mission to empower vulnerable youth and children in Tanzania. To maximaze its impact, Hananasi orphanage center has built its first boarding secondary school in Mkuranga region. Hananasif Orphanage School is a boarding secondary school in Mkuranga region of Tanzania.

HOCET is the registered organization serving orphaned and vulnerable children in Tanzania. It started its operation in 2002 by providing daily care to the kids around Kinondoni ward in Dar es Salaam. By then it was serving tenth of vulnerable kids. Currently hundreds of children have been served by this organization in terms of moral and material support. The organisation has two centers namely HOCET CITY CENTRE which is now serving as dropping center whereas kids are staying transitionally while waiting to join HOCET SECONDARY SCHOOL which is under Hananasif Orphanage Centre; located at Mkuranga district in Coast Region.

The school is pioneering the right to quality education, and skills development for out of school vulnerable youth of Tanzania. The boarding school registers 50 beneficiaries every year for the intevation of four years. Each year, our school takes 70% female beneficiaries. Due to the number of female students. Our school accomodation facilities are not enough. Hense the management of Hocet seek to mobile resources for the female domitory construction. The domitory expected to accomodate 80 female student beneficiries.

Under the guidance of Hezekia Mwalugaja who is the CEO and the founder, the organization is currently serving around 170 kids (including secondary school & higher education) whereby at least quarter of them are phasing out every year while quarter of them are getting enrolled yearly.

VISION

>> A Tanzania in which orphaned and vulnerable kids are integrated into a healthy, caring, and dynamic community and given tools they need to establish themselves as agents of change in their own communities including school by putting life in their days, not the days in their lives.

MISSION

>> To give kids in Tanzania an opportunity to create, learn and develop talents within a caring and nurturing environment.
