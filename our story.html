OUR SUCCESS STORIES

STORY No. 1

YOUTH INITIATED INNOVATIVE AND SUSTAINABLE PROGRAMS

HOCET has been shaping youths to design, initiate and manage various projects in order to give them great potential for self-employment in the future. But also the produce from these projects have been used at our school, for instance vegetables are eaten by students themselves. Once sold the revenue has been used to meet their needs, including stationeries, shoes and paying for health insurance.

Also,the school uniforms of HOCET students are 100% sewn by the students themselves

STORY No. 2

YOUTH INITIATED INNOVATIVE AND SUSTAINABLE PROGRAMS

>> This is an interesting success story. Anna J., who never knew her parents, was handed over to HOCET by the Local Government when she was still very young. HOCET raised and educated this girl.

>> Right now, after finishing his studies and getting a job in USA, This beautiful girl has started building a girls' dormitory at the HOCET school.

STORY No. 3

SERVED TO SERVE OTHERS

Some University graduates have returned home (HOCET) to help teaching their younger brothers and sisters.

This is the great spirit of love and kindness.

These graduates are very helpful in curbing the shortage teachers at HOCET.

STORY No. 4

MAKING THE ROLE MODELS

HOCET along with nurturing and empowering young people to get formal education, has been at the forefront of building these children in moral development, self- awareness and fear of God.

These young people after finishing their studies and starting life have become role models by tying their marriages in the church after following all the divine procedures
